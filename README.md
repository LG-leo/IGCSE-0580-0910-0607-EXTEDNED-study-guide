# 0580 IGCSE Mathematics Syllabus (Extended highlighted)

## 1. 数 (Number)

### 1.1 数的类型 (Types of number)
- 自然数、整数、质数、平方数、立方数  
  Natural numbers, integers, prime numbers, square numbers, cube numbers
- 公因数、公倍数  
  Common factors, common multiples
- 有理数、无理数、倒数  
  Rational and irrational numbers, reciprocals

### 1.2 集合 (Sets)
- 集合语言与符号  
  Set language and notation: $\in$, $\notin$, $\subset$, $\supset$, $\cup$, $\cap$, $A'$, $\varnothing$, $U$
- 文氏图（二至三个集合）  
  Venn diagrams (two or three sets)
- 描述法  
  Set‑builder notation: $\{x: \text{condition}\}$

### 1.3 幂与根 (Powers and roots)
- 平方、平方根、立方、立方根  
  Squares, square roots, cubes, cube roots
- 指数定律（正整数、零、负整数、分数指数）  
  Laws of indices (positive, zero, negative, fractional exponents)
- 计算含幂的表达式  
  Calculate with powers and roots

### 1.4 分数、小数、百分数 (Fractions, decimals and percentages)
- 分数化简、假分数与带分数互化  
  Simplify fractions, convert between improper fractions and mixed numbers
- 小数与分数互化（包括循环小数）  
  Convert between decimals and fractions (including recurring decimals)
- 百分数应用：百分比变化、反向百分比  
  Percentage change, reverse percentages

### 1.5 比例与比率 (Ratio and proportion)
- 化简比、按比例分配  
  Simplify ratios, divide a quantity in a given ratio
- 正比与反比（代数表示）  
  Direct and inverse proportion (expressed algebraically)
- 地图比例尺、货币换算、速率  
  Map scales, currency conversion, rates (speed, density, pressure, etc.)

### 1.6 指数与标准形式 (Indices and standard form)
- 标准形式  
  Standard form
- 计算含标准形式的表达式  
  Calculate with numbers in standard form

### 1.7 估计与近似 (Estimation and approximation)
- 四舍五入（小数位、有效数字）  
  Rounding to a given number of decimal places or significant figures
- 估算复杂计算  
  Estimate the value of a calculation
- 上下界（给定精度时）  
  Upper and lower bounds (when data is rounded)

### 1.8 百分比应用 (Percentages)
- 单利、复利、折旧、人口变化  
  Simple interest, compound interest, depreciation, population change
- 连续百分比变化  
  Repeated percentage change

### 1.9 指数增长与衰减 (Exponential growth and decay)
- 模型 $y = ab^x$  
  Model exponential growth and decay

### 1.10 根式 (Surds)
- 化简根式  
  Simplify surds
- 分母有理化  
  Rationalise the denominator

## 2. 代数与图像 (Algebra and graphs)

### 2.1 代数表达式 (Algebraic manipulation)
- 合并同类项、展开括号、提取公因式  
  Collect like terms, expand brackets, factorise by taking out common factors
- 因式分解：平方差、完全平方、三项式、分组分解  
  Factorise: difference of squares, perfect square, quadratic trinomials, grouping
- 配方  
  Complete the square

### 2.2 代数分式 (Algebraic fractions)
- 加减乘除分式  
  Add, subtract, multiply and divide algebraic fractions
- 化简分式（含因式分解）  
  Simplify rational expressions (using factorisation)

### 2.3 方程 (Equations)
- 解线性方程、分数方程  
  Solve linear equations, fractional equations
- 联立方程（线性+线性，线性+二次）  
  Solve simultaneous equations (linear/linear, linear/quadratic)
- 二次方程：因式分解、配方、公式法  
  Solve quadratic equations by factorising, completing the square, using the quadratic formula
- 解含参方程  
  Solve equations involving parameters

### 2.4 不等式 (Inequalities)
- 解一元一次、二次不等式，在数轴上表示  
  Solve linear and quadratic inequalities, represent on a number line
- 解线性不等式组，用图表示可行域  
  Solve linear inequalities in two variables graphically, shade unwanted regions

### 2.5 数列 (Sequences)
- 找规律，写出通项  
  Find patterns, write the $n$th term
- 线性数列、二次数列、指数数列  
  Linear, quadratic and exponential sequences
- 递推关系  
  Term‑to‑term rules

### 2.6 比例与函数 (Proportion and functions)
- 直接与反比例  
  Direct and inverse proportion
- 用代数式表示比例关系  
  Express proportional relationships algebraically

### 2.7 函数 (Functions)
- 函数定义、定义域、值域，符号 $f(x)$  
  Definition of a function, domain, range, function notation
- 复合函数 $fg(x)$，反函数 $f^{-1}(x)$  
  Composite functions, inverse functions
- 函数的图像：线性、二次、三次、反比例、指数  
  Graphs of linear, quadratic, cubic, reciprocal, exponential functions
- 渐近线（垂直、水平）  
  Asymptotes (vertical, horizontal)

### 2.8 图像变换 (Graph transformations)
- 平移  
  Translations
- 伸缩（不要求具体公式，但应理解图像变化）  
  Stretches (understanding of effect)

### 2.9 微积分初步（求导‑基础） (Introduction to calculus – differentiation basic)
- 导数是变化率  
  Derivative as a rate of change
- 对 $x^n$ 求导（$n$ 为整数、分数）  
  Differentiate $x^n$ ($n$ integer or fraction)
- 多项式函数求导  
  Differentiate polynomial functions
- 应用：切线斜率、切线方程、驻点、最值问题  
  Applications: gradient of tangent, equation of tangent, stationary points (max/min), simple optimisation
- 限制：不涉及链式法则、积商法则、三角函数、指数对数求导  
  Limitations: no chain rule, product/quotient rule, derivatives of trig/exponential/log functions

## 3. 坐标几何 (Coordinate geometry)
- 两点间距离、中点  
  Distance between two points, midpoint
- 直线斜率、直线方程（$y = mx + c$，$ax + by = c$）  
  Gradient, equation of a straight line
- 平行与垂直的条件  
  Condition for parallel and perpendicular lines
- 求直线与曲线交点  
  Find intersection of line and curve

---

## 4. 几何 (Geometry)

### 4.1 几何术语与性质 (Geometrical terms)
- 点、线、面、平行、垂直、角（锐角、钝角、反射角）  
  Point, line, plane, parallel, perpendicular, acute/obtuse/reflex angle
- 三角形分类、特殊四边形、多边形、圆各部分名称  
  Types of triangle, special quadrilaterals, polygons, parts of a circle

### 4.2 几何构造 (Geometrical constructions)
- 用尺规作三角形（已知三边）  
  Construct a triangle given three sides
- 画展开图（立方体、棱柱、棱锥）  
  Draw nets of cubes, prisms, pyramids

### 4.3 相似与全等 (Similarity and congruence)
- 相似三角形判定（AA、SAS、SSS）  
  Criteria for similarity (AA, SAS, SSS)
- 相似图形的长度、面积、体积比例  
  Relationship between lengths, areas and volumes of similar figures

### 4.4 对称 (Symmetry)
- 轴对称、旋转对称（阶数）  
  Line symmetry, rotational symmetry (order)
- 立体图形的对称面  
  Planes of symmetry in 3D solids

### 4.5 角度计算 (Angle properties)
- 点、线、平行线、三角形、四边形内角和  
  Angles at a point, on a line, parallel lines, angle sum of triangle/quadrilateral
- 多边形内角和外角和  
  Interior and exterior angles of polygons

### 4.6 圆定理 (Circle theorems)
- 圆心角定理、圆周角定理、半圆上的圆周角  
  Angle at centre, angle in semicircle, angles in same segment
- 弦心距、切线性质、切弦角（alternate segment）  
  Perpendicular bisector of chord, tangent‑radius, alternate segment theorem
- 四点共圆（对角互补）  
  Cyclic quadrilateral (opposite angles supplementary)

### 4.7 向量 (Vectors)
- 二维向量表示 $\begin{pmatrix}x\\y\end{pmatrix}$  
  Represent vectors in 2D
- 向量加减、数乘、模长  
  Addition, subtraction, scalar multiplication, magnitude
- 位置向量、向量几何（平行、共线、分点）  
  Position vectors, geometric problems (parallel, collinear, ratio points)

## 5. 度量 (Mensuration)
- 单位换算（面积、体积）  
  Conversion of units (area, volume)
- 周长、面积：矩形、三角形、平行四边形、梯形、圆  
  Perimeter and area of rectangle, triangle, parallelogram, trapezium, circle
- 弧长、扇形面积  
  Arc length, sector area
- 表面积、体积：立方体、棱柱、圆柱、圆锥、球、棱锥  
  Surface area and volume of cube, prism, cylinder, cone, sphere, pyramid

---

## 6. 三角学 (Trigonometry)

### 6.1 直角三角形 (Right‑angled triangles)
- 勾股定理  
  Pythagoras’ theorem
- 正弦、余弦、正切（SOH CAH TOA）  
  Sine, cosine, tangent
- 仰角、俯角、方位角  
  Angles of elevation/depression, bearings

### 6.2 精确值 (Exact trigonometric values)
- 记忆 $0^\circ, 30^\circ, 45^\circ, 60^\circ, 90^\circ$ 的三角函数值  
  Exact values of $\sin$, $\cos$, $\tan$ for $0^\circ,30^\circ,45^\circ,60^\circ,90^\circ$ angles

### 6.3 任意三角形 (Non‑right‑angled triangles)
- 正弦定理、余弦定理、面积公式  
  Sine rule, cosine rule, area formula
- 应用：解三角形，包括钝角和 ambiguous case  
  Solve triangles, including obtuse angles and ambiguous case

### 6.4 三角函数图像 (Graphs of trigonometric functions)
- 绘制并解读图表  
  Sketch and interpret graphs
- 解三角方程  
  Solve simple trigonometric equations

### 6.5 三维三角 (Trigonometry in 3D)
- 在立体图形中应用勾股定理和三角函数求长度、角度（线与面的夹角）  
  Apply Pythagoras and trig in 3D (angle between line and plane)

## 7. 变换与向量 (Transformations and vectors)
- 反射、旋转、平移、缩放（包括负和分数缩放因子）  
  Reflection, rotation, translation, enlargement (including negative and fractional scale factors)
- 向量运算与几何证明  
  Vector operations and geometric proofs

## 8. 概率 (Probability)
- 概率尺度（0 到 1）  
  Probability scale
- 符号 $P(A), P(A'), P(A \cup B), P(A \cap B)$  
  Probability notation
- 相对频率、期望频率  
  Relative frequency, expected frequency
- 组合事件：样本空间、文氏图、树图（有放回/无放回）  
  Combined events: sample space diagrams, Venn diagrams, tree diagrams (with/without replacement)
- 条件概率（用树图或表，不要求符号）  
  Conditional probability (using tree diagrams or tables, notation not required)

## 9. 统计 (Statistics)
- 数据分类与制表  
  Classify and tabulate data
- 平均数、中位数、众数、极差、四分位数、四分位距  
  Mean, median, mode, range, quartiles, interquartile range
- 频数分布表、估算均值（分组数据）  
  Frequency tables, estimate mean of grouped data
- 统计图：条形图、饼图、茎叶图、散点图、累积频数图、直方图  
  Bar charts, pie charts, stem‑and‑leaf diagrams, scatter diagrams, cumulative frequency graphs, histograms
- 从累积频数图读取中位数、百分位数  
  Read median, percentiles from cumulative frequency graph
- 相关性与最佳拟合直线  
  Correlation, line of best fit
